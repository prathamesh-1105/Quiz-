💡 How to paste from ChatGPT

Ask ChatGPT to generate multiple choice questions, then paste them here. Standard formats are parsed automatically:

1. What is the capital of India?
A) Mumbai
B) New Delhi
C) Kolkata
D) Chennai
Answer: B

Options can start with A-D, a-d, or 1-4. The answer line can start with Answer:, Correct Option:, Ans:, etc.

      🚀 Host Your Quiz App Online (Free & Easy)

      To enable seamless automatic syncing between your PC and your students' devices, the app must be loaded from a web address (using https://) rather than a local file (file://).

      You can host this entire app for free on GitHub Pages in under 2 minutes:

      1. Go to github.com and sign up for a free account.
      2. Click New Repository. Name it quiz, make it Public, and click Create repository.
      3. Click the "uploading an existing file" link on the repository page.
      4. Drag and drop your local index.html, script.js, and style.css files, then click Commit changes.
      5. Go to repository Settings → Pages tab. Under Build and deployment, set the Branch to main and click Save.
      💡 That's it! Your quiz app will be live at https://your-username.github.io/quiz/. Give this link to your students, and your quizzes will sync instantly across all devices!
